GoOffGrid — Gizlilik Politikasi

Son guncelleme: 10 Nisan 2026

GoOffGrid ("biz", "bizim" veya "uygulama"), kullanicilarin dijital detoks deneyimine deger verir ve gizliligini korumaya baglidir. Bu politika hangi verileri topladigimizi, neden topladigimizi ve nasil kullandigimizi aciklar.

1. Topladigimiz Veriler

  • Hesap bilgileri: ad, e-posta, profil fotografi (isteginize bagli)
  • Kullanim verileri: oturum suresi, hedefler, basarim ilerlemesi
  • Cihaz izinleri:
    • Kamera: hikaye ve profil fotografi olusturmak icin (yalnizca kullanici baslattiginda)
    • Konum: sehir siralamasi ve ganimet haritasi icin (isteginize bagli)
    • Bildirimler: seans hatirlatici ve sosyal bildirim icin

2. Verileri Nasil Kullaniriz

  • Uygulama ozelliklerini saglamak (siralamalar, oturumlar, sosyal etkilesim)
  • Hesabinizi yonetmek ve guvenlik saglamak
  • Urunu iyilestirmek icin toplu (anonim) analitik

3. Veri Paylasimi

Kisisel verilerinizi satmayiz. Verileriniz yalnizca uygulama islevlerini saglamak icin asagidaki servis saglayicilarla paylasilir:

  • Supabase (kimlik dogrulama ve veritabani)
  • Google Play Services (bildirimler)
  • RevenueCat (abonelik yonetimi)

4. Veri Saklama

Verileriniz hesabiniz aktif oldugu surece saklanir. Hesabi silmek istediginizde tum verileriniz 30 gun icinde kalici olarak silinir.

5. Haklariniz

KVKK ve GDPR cercevesinde verilerinize erisme, duzeltme, silme ve tasima hakkina sahipsiniz. Talepler icin: privacy@gooffgrid.app

6. Cocuklarin Gizliligi

GoOffGrid 13 yas altina yonelik degildir ve bilerek 13 yas altindan veri toplamayiz.

7. Degisiklikler

Bu politikayi zaman zaman guncelleyebiliriz. Onemli degisiklikler uygulama icinden bildirilir.

8. Iletisim

Sorulariniz icin: privacy@gooffgrid.app

GoOffGrid — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

GoOffGrid ("we", "our", or "the app") values users' digital detox experience and is committed to protecting privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, profile photo (optional)
  • Usage data: session duration, goals, achievement progress
  • Device permissions:
    • Camera: to create stories and profile photos (only when user initiates)
    • Location: for city ranking and loot map (optional)
    • Notifications: for session reminders and social updates

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide app features (rankings, sessions, social interactions)
  • To manage your account and ensure security
  • Aggregate anonymous analytics to improve the product

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with service providers to deliver app functionality:

  • Supabase (authentication and database)
  • Google Play Services (notifications)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

4. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and KVKK, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data. For requests: privacy@gooffgrid.app

6. Children's Privacy

GoOffGrid is not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be announced in-app.

8. Contact

Questions: privacy@gooffgrid.app
