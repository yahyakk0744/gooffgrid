Full Description:
-----------------
Quitting your phone is hard. Quitting alone is even harder.

gooffgrid tracks your screen time, builds real digital detox habits, and turns the whole thing into a friendly — but competitive — experience with your friends.

🏆 DUEL SYSTEM
Challenge a friend: "Who uses their phone less this week?" The winner earns a badge and bragging rights. The loser owes an apology. Once your friend sends that duel request, you can't just walk away.

📊 SCREEN TIME TRACKING
See exactly how many hours you spend on your phone each day and which apps are eating your time. Track weekly and monthly trends. Awareness is the first step to change.

🥇 LEADERBOARDS
Compete in weekly screen time rankings with your friends. This time, the least addicted wins. Dominate the list — and prove that digital discipline is your superpower.

📖 STORIES
Share your daily detox wins. "I stayed phone-free for 4 hours today." Inspire others, get inspired. Build your digital health together with your community.

🌬 BREATHING EXERCISES
When you feel the urge to grab your phone, stop and breathe. Guided breathing exercises help you manage in-the-moment stress, break the addiction cycle, and clear your mind.

🎯 PERSONAL GOALS
Set daily and weekly screen time targets. The app gently alerts you as you approach your limit and celebrates every win with you.

🏅 BADGE SYSTEM
Earn badges for every milestone: "3-day streak", "Weekly duel champion", "Detox master", "100 breaths completed", and many more. Show them off on your profile.

🔔 REAL-TIME NOTIFICATIONS
Get notified when you're approaching your daily limit. Stay aware of your progress all day long.

WHY gooffgrid?

Most digital detox apps are boring. You set a goal, try for a few days, then give up. gooffgrid is different: social competition is built into the core. You're not alone. You're fighting alongside your friends.

Phone addiction is a real problem. But the solution is just as real: community, competition, breathing, and small daily wins.

Leave the grid. Come back to life.
